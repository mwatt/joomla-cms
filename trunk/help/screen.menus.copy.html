Menus: Copy Menu Items

-- Draft Version 4.5.3

This page lets copy one or multiple Menu Items to another existing Menu.  If you choose to copy a Parent Menu Item and its sub-level menus, they will all be copied together keeping their Parent-child relationship.

Copy to Menu:
This drop-down displays a list of your existing Menus as displayed through the Menu Manager.
You may choose there the Menu you want the Menu Item(s) to be copied to.

Menu Items being copied:
This shows a list of the Menu Items being copied.

Toolbar:
Copy: Click this icon to copy the Menu Items.
Cancel: Brings back to the Menu you are editing.
