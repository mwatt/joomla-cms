Export Manager

draft 4.5.3

The Export Manager lets create a dump of your database.
The dump is created in the administrator/components/com_export/files folder.

Toolbar Icons

Export: Once the parameters for the dump are chosen in the Options area, click this icon to save the dump.

Options

Select Table(s): lets select all or specific tables to export.

Source: Structure, data or both.

Format: Choose here the appropriate format.

Output: The dump may be displayed on screen or as text, TAR or GZIP compressed file.
