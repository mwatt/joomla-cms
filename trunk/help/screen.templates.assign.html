Templates: Assign

--- Draft 4.5.3

This page lets you assign the template chosen to specific Menu Items/pages.

The list displays all your Menu Items grouped per menu.
 
You may highlight here multiple items.
This will override de default template for the pages chosen.

If you uncheck all, the Default template will be used for all  Menu Items/pages.

Toolbar Icons


Save: Saves your choice and returns to the Template Manager.

Cancel: Cancels the action and returns to the Template Manager.
