Messages Inbox

-- Draft 4.5.3

The Private Messaging Manager displays your current Inbox and lets you send new messages.
Private messaging is only supported to or between Users in a group that has access to the Admin Section.

Toolbar Icons

Select items by clicking the checkbox at the start of each row.

New: Opens a new window to type a new message and choose the recipient among the back-end users.

Delete: Deletes messages from the Inbox. These messages are not recoverable through the trash.

Filters

From left to right:
Search a Message by name.

Column Descriptions

Clicking on any title will order the items according to the title clicked.

To view a message, click the hyperlinked 'Subject' of the desired message. Details of the message will be shown. Click the 'Back' icon in the toolbar to return to your Inbox.
 
To create a new message, click the 'New' icon in the toolbar. Select the Recipient from the list and enter the desired subject and Message. Click the 'Send' icon to send the message. Click the 'Cancel' icon to abort the operation.
 
To delete a message, select the checkbox next to the Subject of the message(s) that you want to delete. Click the 'Delete' icon in the toolbar. An alert box will ask you to confirm the operation.
 
At the bottom of the list you may see a pagination feature, which will allow you to navigate to the next set of items in the list. You can also set the number of items displayed on a page by selecting the number in the drop down box at the bottom of the screen.
