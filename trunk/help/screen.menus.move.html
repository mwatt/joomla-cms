Menus: Move Menu Items

-- Draft Version 4.5.3

This page lets move one or multiple Menu Items to another existing Menu. If you choose to move a Parent Menu Item and its sub-level menus, they will all move together keeping their Parent-child relationship. 

Move to Menu:
This drop-down displays a list of your existing Menus as displayed through the Menu Manager.
You may choose there the Menu you want the Menu Item(s) to be moved to.

Menu Items being moved:
This shows a list of the Menu Items being moved.

Toolbar:
Move: Click this icon to move the Menu Items.
Cancel: Brings back to the Menu you are editing.
