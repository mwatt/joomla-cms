Modules: New

4.5.3

The Modules: New screen is displayed when you click on the "New" Toolbar icon in the Module Manager.

It displays a list of the Modules installed for the Site (Front-end) or Administrator (Back-end) parts of your Joomla! installation.

Please choose a Module by checking the radio-button next to it and clicking on the "New" icon in the Toobar.
Alternatively, you may just click on the name of the Module.

A "New Module"  screen will be displayed where you may edit the details and parameters concerning the module just chosen.
