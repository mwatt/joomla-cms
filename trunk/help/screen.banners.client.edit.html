--- Draft 4.5.3

This page displays information about a client when you create or edit it.

Toolbar Icons

Save: Click here to save your new or edited client's details. Returns to the Client Manager screen.

Apply: Click here to save your new or edited client's details. Keeps the New/edit screen displayed for further editing.

Cancel: Cancels the action. Any modification is lost. If opened to edit, the client remains checked-in.

Details

Client Name: Type here the name of the business which buys banner display from your site.

Contact Name: Type here the name of whom to contact about matters related to this client.

Contact Email: TYpe here the e-mail of the person to contact. Will only be visible here.

Extra Info: Type here any other information about the client you wish to store. Only visible here.
